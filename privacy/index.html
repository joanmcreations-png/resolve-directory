Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

resolve.directory ("we", "the site") is a free directory of PowerGrades and LUTs for DaVinci Resolve. This page explains what data we collect and how it's used.

What we collect

  • If you sign in with Google: your name, email address, and profile picture, plus anything you add yourself (country, editing-since year, company/studio, social link).
  • If you subscribe to "Get notified": your email address.
  • If you submit a resource: the link you send us.

How we use it

  • To show your profile if you choose to fill one in.
  • To email you about new resources or site updates, only if you signed up or signed in.
  • To review and add resources people submit.

We don't sell your data, and we don't run ads on this site.

Third-party services we use

  • Google — for signing in.
  • Supabase — to store accounts and profile data.
  • Resend — to send emails to people who signed up for updates.
  • Formspree — to receive submitted resources and notification sign-ups.

Your choices

You can stop receiving emails at any time — just tell us via the "Submit a Resource" form and we'll remove you. Signing out of your Google account on this site doesn't delete your profile data; ask us and we'll delete it.

Changes

We may update this policy as the site grows. Material changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date above.
