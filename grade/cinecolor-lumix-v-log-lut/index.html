CineColor Lumix V-Log LUT

FREE

Free Panasonic Lumix V-Log conversion LUT, cropped and full-frame versions.

LUT Pack
Visit resource
How to install this in DaVinci Resolve

Go to Project Settings → Color Management → Open LUT Folder, then copy the downloaded .cube file(s) into that folder. Restart DaVinci Resolve, right-click a clip on the Color page, and the LUT will appear under 3D LUTs in the panel on the right.
