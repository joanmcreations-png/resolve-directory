Frequently asked

Are these LUTs really free?

Yes. Every LUT listed here is offered free by its creator. Some ask for an email or a social follow, but none cost money.

What format are the LUTs?

Almost all are .cube files, the standard 3D LUT format DaVinci Resolve reads natively. A few also include .xmp or .look versions.

Do these work in the free version of Resolve?

Yes. 3D LUTs work identically in DaVinci Resolve and DaVinci Resolve Studio, so the free version handles all of them.
