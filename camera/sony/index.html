Frequently asked

How do I convert Sony log footage in DaVinci Resolve?

Start with an official Sony S-Log3 / S-Gamut3 to Rec.709 conversion LUT to get a neutral, correct base, then build your creative grade on top of it.

Are these Sony LUTs free?

Yes — every Sony LUT and grade listed here is free to download from its creator or from the manufacturer.

What format are they?

Mostly .cube 3D LUTs, which DaVinci Resolve reads natively in both the free and Studio versions.
